Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase books from PageTurner?

+

Browse our catalog, select your desired book format (e-book, paperback, or hardcover), add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal for secure payment processing.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for physical books in new condition. E-books are non-refundable once downloaded. If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days for a full refund or replacement.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional fee. E-books are delivered instantly upon purchase confirmation. International shipping times vary by location.

Can I access my e-books on multiple devices?

+

Yes! Your e-book purchases are linked to your account and can be accessed on up to 5 devices simultaneously. Simply log in to your account on any device to download and read your books.

Do you offer gift cards or gift wrapping?

+

Yes, we offer both digital and physical gift cards in various denominations. Gift wrapping is available during checkout for physical books at a small additional cost. Perfect for birthdays, holidays, and special occasions!

How do I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also log in to your account and view order status in the "My Orders" section. Track your package in real-time until it arrives!
